Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Emma Richardson, Emmas Iconic Style began as a small boutique in downtown Chicago with a simple mission: to provide women with stylish, comfortable footwear that doesn't sacrifice quality for fashion.

What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved brand with multiple locations across the Midwest and a thriving online store serving customers nationwide.

Every shoe in our collection is carefully selected by Emma and our team to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, comfort, and timeless design. We believe every woman deserves to feel confident and comfortable in her shoes.

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, in original condition, and in their original packaging. We provide detailed size charts for each product to help you find your perfect fit. If you're between sizes or unsure, we recommend sizing up. You can also visit one of our stores for a professional fitting. Don't hesitate to reach out to our customer service team with specific sizing questions about any style.
